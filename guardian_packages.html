Guardian Packages


Designed for Landlords and commercial building owners

At Naxco we pride ourselves on our pioneering approach to taking care of our customers full property needs. That is why we have created our Guardian Packages. Each package is tailored with a complete property survey and our checklist. From this we create a maintenance plan that would be suited to your property needs. You can be a landlord with multiple properties and want to have a management and maintenance that you can see value in, or a homeowner that wants the reassurance. With our Guardian packages you can either choose an off the shelf package and then have bolt on services if required.


Standard Guardian Package

Book in a planned maintenance. Fixed rate for trades after the 6 hours PA. Schedule the cleaning cycles for the Guttering and Gas & Electrical annual checks. Fixed rate for trades.

Advanced Guardian Package

Book in a planned maintenance. Schedule the cleaning cycles for the Gutters and Gas & Electrical annual checks. Fixed rate for trades after the 16 hours PA. 16 hours of work included per year – This can be used in any way the customer chooses. We intend it to be used for customers to have a safety net that they can call and not be charged, and this ensures they Asset/Property is looked after. It removes the little niggles of managing a property portfolio or for scheduled work for a homeowner.

Ultimate Guardian Package

This is for specific tailored solutions to Councils, Care homes, blocks of apartments, Offices Draft property Inventories, Book in a planned maintenance. Schedule the cleaning cycles for the Gutters and Gas & Electrical annual checks Creating tailored plans and schedules for the life of the contract to increase the value of the asset.
